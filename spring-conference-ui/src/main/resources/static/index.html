Toronto Spring Conference
September 29, 2016

On September 29th, 2016, from 8:00am to 6:00pm, Pivotal will be hosting a Toronto Spring Conference in downtown Toronto. This is a full day event, starting at 8am and finishing at 6pm, with over 10 sessions from Spring committers and thought leaders. Hear from Juergen Hoeller (Co Founder and project lead of the Spring Framework), Dr David Syer (Founder of Spring Boot, Spring Cloud and committer on countless Spring projects) Rossen Stoyanchev (Spring MVC committer with a focus on "reactive"), Spencer Gibb (Spring Cloud Committer), Marius Bogoevici (Spring Cloud Stream lead and Spring Cloud Data Flow committer), Kris De Volder (Spring Tool Suite committer) and more.
